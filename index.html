About

I'm a reliability engineer with GE Aerospace, currently working on RM&T systems in the Power and Avionics business. My work sits at the intersection of engineering and data analysis, whether that's reliability modeling, FMEA, or building tools that make these methods more accessible.

Over the years I've developed several software packages for reliability, risk, and data analysis, and I'm active in the research and development community. Outside of work I volunteer as a guest speaker for STEM in virtual classrooms, contribute to NASA citizen science programs, and spent a few years as a volunteer firefighter.
